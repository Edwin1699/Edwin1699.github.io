The Details
Gundam Wing, one of the most beloved Gundam's of the Western Hemisphere and first broadcasted on American television. Gundam Wing would be one of the first Gundams taking place outside of the original Gundam timeline, taking place in the After War years. Gundam Wing would see itself be destroyed, the only Gundam to resemble its ancestor Wing Zero Gundam. Wing Zero would take control of the series, which would later be completed into Endless Waltz Wing Zero Gundam, its strongest and final "form". This Gundam would be piloted by a teenage super soldier Heero Yuy in a fight to protect Earth from his partners older brother, Milliardo PeaceCraft who pilots the Gundam Epyon.
