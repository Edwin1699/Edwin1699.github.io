The Details
As one of the oddest Gundam series, also separating itself from the main timeline (Universal Century), Gundam 00 takes its series into action against "innovators", a race of super human beings who puppet the first season of the series in the background perfectly. The pilot himself is an innovator, a pure one who joins Celestial Being to pilot 00 Gundam. His name is Setsuna F. Seiei and his one wish is to eradicate war.
